# Design System: Tastify Ultra-Minimalist

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
"Pure Utility." A shadowless, border-based interface that relies on perfect spacing and a single, high-quality sans-serif typeface. No fancy serifs, no italics, no flashy animations. The atmosphere is neutral, professional, and dead-simple.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Canvas:** `#FBFBFA` (Off-white)
- **Ink:** `#111111` (Near-black)
- **Muted:** `#71717A` (Zinc-500)
- **Border:** `#EAEAEA` (Light gray)

## 3. Typography (PURE SANS)
- **Primary Stack:** `Geist Sans`, `Public Sans`, or `system-ui`.
- **Rules:** No Serif fonts. No Italics. Use weight (400, 600) for hierarchy.
- **Sizes:** Keep it standard. Headlines at 2rem max for most pages.

## 4. Components
- **Buttons:** Sharp corners (4px), solid Ink background, Bone text.
- **Cards:** White background, 1px Border, no shadows.
- **Icons:** Thin strokes (1px).

## 5. Anti-Patterns (BANNED)
- No Serif fonts (Newsreader, Playfair, etc.).
- No Italics in UI text.
- No Shadows.
- No flashy gradients.
- No complex layout overlaps.
