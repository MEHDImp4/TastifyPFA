<objective>
Migrate the Just-In-Time (JIT) stock deduction from a synchronous blocking process to an asynchronous background task.

Purpose: Improve order creation latency and system responsiveness under high load.
Output: Celery task for stock deduction and updated StockService.
</objective>

<feature>
  <name>Async Stock Deduction</name>

    - Behavior: 
        1. Fetch Plat and invoke `StockService.deduct_ingredients_for_plat`.

  </behavior>
  <implementation>

  </implementation>
</feature>
